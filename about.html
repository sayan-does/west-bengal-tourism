About West Bengal

West Bengal, located in the eastern part of India, is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and diverse landscapes. From the bustling city of Kolkata to the serene hills of Darjeeling, West Bengal offers something for every traveler.

Explore the state's vibrant traditions, delicious cuisine, and iconic landmarks.
